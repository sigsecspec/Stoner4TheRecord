## Concept

Create a podcast that mimics a radio station but includes other segment types woven throughout the show.

## Key Rules

- **All sponsor segments are live** — no prerecorded spots. Allow **8-minute slots**.
- **Sponsor placement:** always after the host, before the DJ (or next entertainment block).
- **Pre-show:** guest DJ plays during stream load-in, before the opening host.
- **Chit Chat Smoke Sesh** is the heart of the show — the host breaks always pitch back to it.
- **Comedy Corner** sits between music and games for a change of pace.
- **Guests** can be one continuous co-host for the whole show, or alternate guests throughout.
