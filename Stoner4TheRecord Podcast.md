# Overview

Stoner4TheRecord is a live cannabis culture entertainment network built around the feel of a traditional radio station while embracing modern livestreaming, podcasting, and community interaction.

Every broadcast combines live DJs, comedy, games, interviews, cannabis culture, independent music, community discussions, and live sponsor spotlights into one continuous experience.

The show is designed so that no matter when someone tunes in, there is always something happening.

# Broadcast Philosophy

Stoner4TheRecord isn't recorded like a traditional podcast.

It is programmed like an internet radio station.

Every segment naturally flows into the next while the audience remains part of the conversation from beginning to end.

The goal is simple:

> The stream should never feel empty.

Whether someone joins thirty minutes early or halfway through the show, they immediately become part of the experience.

---

# Programming Structure

## 🎧 Guest DJ Warm-Up (Pre-Show)

**15–30 Minutes**

The stream begins before the official show.

As viewers begin joining:

- Guest DJ performs live
- Countdown timer
- Live chat opens
- Station branding
- Sponsor banners
- Upcoming guests
- Community announcements
- Social media highlights
- "Now Playing"

This creates the feeling of tuning into a real radio station already on the air.

---

# 🌿 Chit Chat Smoke Sesh

## The Heart of Stoner4TheRecord

Every major segment begins and ends here. The host is the anchor of the show — every host break pitches back to the Smoke Sesh.

The Chit Chat Smoke Sesh is where the audience hangs out with the host.

Nothing is overly scripted.

Conversation comes naturally.

The host guides the discussion while interacting with guests and viewers.

Topics may include:

- Cannabis
- Music
- Funny stories
- Community questions
- Current events
- Viewer call-ins
- Food
- Movies
- Gaming
- Sports
- Local events
- Business
- Entrepreneurship
- Travel
- Random conversations

# 🎙️ Live Sponsor Spotlight

**8 Minutes**

Every entertainment block is introduced by a live sponsor segment placed **after the host and before the next segment** (DJ set, comedy, or games).

There are **NO prerecorded commercials.**

Sponsors appear live with the host during the Chit Chat Smoke Sesh.

Typical sponsor spotlight:

- Company introduction
- Product demonstration
- Brand story

# Production Rules

### Rule 001

Every major entertainment segment begins with a Chit Chat Smoke Sesh.

---

### Rule 002

Every DJ set is preceded by a Live Sponsor Spotlight.

---

### Rule 003

There are no prerecorded advertisements.

Every sponsor appears live.

---

### Rule 004

The audience should never experience dead air.

Music, conversation, or visuals should always be active.

---

### Rule 005

The Chit Chat Smoke Sesh is the center of every broadcast.

Every segment returns here.

---

### Rule 006

Guests are encouraged to stay for multiple segments whenever possible.

Chemistry creates better entertainment.

---

### Rule 007

The audience is considered another guest on the show.

Chat participation is encouraged at all times.
