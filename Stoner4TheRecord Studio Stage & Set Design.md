# 🎥 Studio Stage & Set Design

## Philosophy

The **Stoner4TheRecord** set is modular.

Every episode can have a different layout depending on the guests, DJs, sponsors, games, or special events.

The goal is to make every broadcast feel fresh while maintaining the station's identity.

The audience should immediately recognize the show regardless of how the furniture is arranged.

# Stage Zones

## 🎧 DJ Booth

Located behind the main stage.

The DJ remains visible throughout the broadcast.

Features:

- Professional DJ Booth
- LED Lighting
- Station Logo
- Moving Lights
- Smoke Effects
- LED Wall
- Visualizers

The DJ becomes part of the atmosphere even while the host is speaking.

---

## 🎙️ Main Stage

This is where all conversations happen.

Furniture changes depending on the show.

No permanent furniture is attached to the stage.

Everything is modular.

---

## 💃 Dance Floor

Located directly in front of the stage.

Purpose:

- Audience dancing
- Giveaways
- Games
- Competitions
- Live performances
- Meet & Greets
- Product demonstrations

This becomes the energy center of the studio.

---

## 👥 Audience Area

Located across from the stage on the opposite side of the dance floor.

Options include:

- Theater seating
- Lounge seating
- Standing audience
- VIP tables
- Bleachers
- Mixed seating

The audience should always be visible on camera.

# Studio Philosophy

The set should never feel locked into one layout.

Furniture should change to match the energy and purpose of each episode while maintaining the same recognizable studio environment.

Whether the host is standing alone, interviewing one guest, hosting a six-person panel, or clearing the stage for a live performance, the audience should always recognize they're watching **Stoner4TheRecord**.

The consistent visual anchors are:

- The elevated DJ booth behind the stage
- The modular main stage
- The dance floor in front of the stage
- The audience surrounding the experience
- The signature Stoner4TheRecord branding displayed throughout the studio
