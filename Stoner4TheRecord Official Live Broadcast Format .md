# Stoner4TheRecord
## Official Live Broadcast Format 

**Broadcast Length:** Approximately 4–4.5 Hours

---

# Pre-Show

## Guest DJ Warm-Up

**Length:** 15–30 Minutes

The stream begins before the official show starts.

While viewers join the stream:

- Guest DJ performs a live mix
- Countdown timer displayed
- Live chat opens
- Sponsor banners rotate
- Upcoming guests are promoted
- Viewer submissions are highlighted
- Station visuals and animations play

This creates the feeling of tuning into a live radio station already in progress.

# Pre-Show Objectives

The Guest DJ Warm-Up is designed to:

- Welcome viewers as they arrive
- Build audience before the official start time
- Create a live radio station atmosphere
- Give guest DJs additional exposure
- Encourage chat participation
- Generate excitement for the upcoming show

---

# During the Warm-Up

Display may include:

- Live countdown timer
- "Now Playing" information
- Guest DJ name
- Sponsor logos
- Upcoming schedule
- QR codes
- Social media handles
- Live chat feed
- Community announcements

---

# Sponsor Placement Rule

Every major entertainment segment follows the same transition:

**Host → Live Sponsor Spotlight → Entertainment**

This ensures consistent pacing while giving sponsors premium placement immediately before featured programming.

---

# Programming Philosophy

Listeners should never join a silent or inactive stream.

Whether someone tunes in 30 minutes early or right at showtime, they should immediately experience live music, visuals, and an active community.

Stoner4TheRecord operates like a modern internet radio station, where the broadcast is already alive before the official show begins.
