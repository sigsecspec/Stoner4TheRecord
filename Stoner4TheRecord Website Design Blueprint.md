# Vision

The website is not just a website.

It is a digital television network, radio station, streaming platform, social community, event center, podcast hub, gaming portal, and cannabis entertainment destination.

When visitors arrive, they should immediately feel like they've walked into a professional broadcast network.

Think...

- ESPN
- Twitch
- Netflix
- Spotify
- YouTube
- iHeartRadio
- SiriusXM

...combined into one experience.
