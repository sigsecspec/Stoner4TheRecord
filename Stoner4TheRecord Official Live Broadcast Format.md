# Stoner4TheRecord
## Official Live Broadcast Format
### Version 1.1

**Website:** https://stoner4therecord.com

**Total Broadcast Length:** Approximately 4–4.5 hours (including pre-show)

**Tagline:** *The Station That Never Stops Smoking.*

---

# Overview

Stoner4TheRecord is programmed like an internet radio station, not a traditional recorded podcast. The stream goes live before the official show starts, and every segment flows naturally into the next. The audience should never experience dead air.

---

# Pre-Show

## Guest DJ Warm-Up

**Length:** 15–30 minutes

The stream begins before the official show. A guest DJ performs live while viewers join.

During warm-up:

- Guest DJ performs a live mix
- Countdown timer displayed
- Live chat opens
- Sponsor banners rotate
- Upcoming guests are promoted
- Viewer submissions are highlighted
- Station visuals and animations play
- "Now Playing" information displayed

This creates the feeling of tuning into a live radio station already in progress.

---

# Official Broadcast Run-of-Show

Every **host segment** is a **Chit Chat Smoke Sesh** — the conversational home base of the show where the host guides discussion, interacts with guests and chat, and bridges every entertainment block.

| Order | Segment | Type | Length |
|------:|---------|------|-------:|
| Pre | Guest DJ Warm-Up | Music | 15–30 min |
| 1 | Opening Chit Chat Smoke Sesh | Host | 15 min |
| 2 | Live Sponsor Spotlight | Sponsor | 8 min |
| 3 | Host DJ Set | Music | 30 min |
| 4 | Chit Chat Smoke Sesh | Host Break | 5 min |
| 5 | Live Sponsor Spotlight | Sponsor | 8 min |
| 6 | Guest DJ Set #1 | Music | 30 min |
| 7 | Chit Chat Smoke Sesh | Host Break | 5 min |
| 8 | Live Sponsor Spotlight | Sponsor | 8 min |
| 9 | Comedy Corner | Comedy | 20 min |
| 10 | Extended Chit Chat Smoke Sesh | Host | 15 min |
| 11 | Live Sponsor Spotlight | Sponsor | 8 min |
| 12 | Stoner Games | Games | 60 min |
| 13 | Chit Chat Smoke Sesh | Host Break | 5 min |
| 14 | Live Sponsor Spotlight | Sponsor | 8 min |
| 15 | Guest DJ Set #2 | Music | 30 min |
| 16 | Closing Chit Chat Smoke Sesh | Host | 15 min |

**Official show runtime:** ~4 hours 6 minutes (excluding pre-show)

> Segment lengths are flexible and may be adjusted per episode. Sponsor slots are always live — never prerecorded.

---

# Sponsor Placement Rule

Every major entertainment segment follows the same transition:

**Chit Chat Smoke Sesh (Host) → Live Sponsor Spotlight → Entertainment**

Sponsors always appear live with the host immediately before DJ sets, comedy, and games. There are **no prerecorded commercials.**

---

# Chit Chat Smoke Sesh — Guest Format

The Smoke Sesh may use any of these guest configurations per episode:

- **Resident Guest** — One guest stays for the entire show as co-host
- **Rotating Guests** — DJs, sponsors, comedians, artists, and community members rotate in and out
- **Open Chair** — An extra seat always available for surprise appearances

---

# Stoner Games Block

The games block runs approximately one hour and may include:

- Stoner Bingo
- Stoner Trivia
- Guess The Strain
- Smoke or Joke
- High or History
- Wheel of Weed
- Smoke Olympics

Viewers participate through the mobile app. See the Production Platform documentation for game system details.

---

# Programming Philosophy

Listeners should never join a silent or inactive stream. Whether someone tunes in 30 minutes early or right at showtime, they should immediately experience live music, conversation, visuals, and an active community.

Stoner4TheRecord operates like a modern internet radio station, where the broadcast is already alive before the official show begins.
